What are the three rules for sounding consonants in verbs?


  1. THEY … - DO always sound the consonant
  2. ..ER Short Box - DO sound the consonant
  3. NOT ..ER Short Box - do NOT sound the consonant

What important distinction must you make between different meanings of the verb “to feel”?


You make a dictinction between “I feel something” and “I feel fine” / “I don’t feel well” / “I feel better”.

The latter translates to “I feel myself fine” etc.
