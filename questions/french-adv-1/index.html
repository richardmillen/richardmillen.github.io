Quiz 20

  1. What are the different verb endings?
  2. What is the most common verb ending?

  4. Give some examples of ir verbs.
  5. Give some examples of re verbs.

  1. There are 4 types:
    1. er
    2. ir
    3. re
    4. oir
  2. er. More than 80%.
  3. parler, manger, demander, rester.
  4. dormir, servir, partir.
  5. vendre, attendre, comprendre, prendre, mettre.
